Reaped: As I am

As I see,
With eyes no longer
I observe,
Unlike ever before
Further and deeper
I behold,
What once I did ignore
I peek,
Weak and meek 
To reality what once was

You see,
Me, as I am
Clear, I shiver,
standing in the sun.
